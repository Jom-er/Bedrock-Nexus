Verdant Gear [Original] Addon

Author: JomGamingPH
Date: July 3, 2025
Version: 1.0.0

Step into the world of Verdant Gear, where time and nature shape your equipment. This addon brings a fresh twist to survival by introducing a complete set of gear that ages through four natural stages — each with its own look, durability, and stats. From polished tools to fully weathered armor, every piece tells a story of endurance, decay, and legacy. Upgrade your gameplay with a deeper sense of progression, all powered by one of Minecraft’s most overlooked materials.

Features:

  • Copper Sword, Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel, and Hoe
  • Full Copper Armor set with high durability
  • Crafting recipes included
  • Balanced damage and defense values
